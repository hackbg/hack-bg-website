hack – Blockchain Development Company joins forces with BlockRocket

BlockRocket and their mission

BLOCKROCKET provides a state of the art ecosystem that helps startups, corporates, and investors to leverage the potential of blockchain technology.

We believe that innovation happens when entrepreneurs and experts from established companies join forces. Therefore, BLOCKROCKET provides a platform based on top-level co-working facilities in Frankfurt, Munich, and Berlin as well as a powerful network of investors, corporates and service providers in the blockchain space. This enables joint projects, knowledge transfer and fundraising opportunities to successfully accelerate blockchain.

How can we help and grow together

Our mission is helping the blockchain space grow. Together with Blockrocket, their network of partners and our community we will be able to support each other’s growth.

Our technical know-how and experience in designing and developing complex blockchain solutions will be the pillar to achieving our common effort to develop a stronger ecosystem.

Why we are happy to be in this community?

As we are involved with both local, regional and international blockchain communities we can help shape a better understanding of blockchain technology while encouraging innovation.

We can feel the synergy between our two organizations and we are thrilled to bring our experience on the table and start creating value.
